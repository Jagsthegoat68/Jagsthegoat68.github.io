Shakey the Robot

Shakey the robot was the first mobile controlled robot by artificial intelligece and it was launched by the SRI internationals. This robot hasis equipped wih sensing devices, and uses a TV camera that senses the surroundings and can get around halls and other walls without really bumping. it can also move at about 2 metres/hour. This robot picks up commands by radio waves.
