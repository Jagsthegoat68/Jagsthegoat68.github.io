Shakey the Robot

Shakey the robot was the first mobile controlled robot by artificial intelligece and it was launched by the SRI internationals. This robot hasis equipped wih sensing devices, and uses a TV camera that senses the surroundings and can get around halls and other walls without really bumping. it can also move at about 2 metres/hour. This robot picks up commands by radio waves.

Ping Pong is released

Ping Pong gets releases in 1972's. The game was tested in bars in Grass Valley and Sunnyvale, California, where it proved very popular. Pong would revolutionize the arcade industry and launch the modern video game era.

The Silver Arm

David Silver at MIT designs the Silver Arm, a robotic arm to do small-parts assembly using feedback from delicate touch and pressure sensors. The arm´s fine movements approximate those of human fingers.

C3PO and R2D2 in star wars

C3PO and R2D2 play a critical role in 1977’s blockbuster hit movie Star Wars.

The Laser Disc

The LaserDisc is introduced as “Discovision” by MCA and Philips. The first LaserDisc sold in North America was the film Jaws.
