NHL Goaltender Interference

There has been a lack of consistency in the reviews for goaltender interference.

Some calls would be utter nonsense including when Brian Dumoulin was pushed into Maple Leafs goalie Frederick Anderson on Saturday Night at the Air Canada Centre. This instance is when Dumoulin was pushing the net with the puck where he is pushed into Frederick Anderson by Ron Hainsey of the Toronto Maple Leafs. The rule shows that contact with the goalie isnot allowed unless someone from the defending team pushes someone into the goaltender. This call has made many people mad including other nhl players including Jamie Benn who went straight to twitter and tweeted "Two minutes for scoring?".

Fleury registers 400th career win

On Monday, March 12th, goalie Marc-Andre Fleury registered his 400th career win against the Philadelphia Flyers. He now is the second fastest goalie to reach 400 career wins only by being beat by Henrik Lundqvist by one day. Fleury is almost guarenteed to be in the top 10 for wins as a goaltender. He is 13th so far but he is only 8 wins away from Grant Fuhr who sits at 10th all time in wins so with 13 games left he would need to win 8 out of those 13 but it will be very unlikely that he gets that many starts as his backup Malcom Subban is in need of playing some games.
