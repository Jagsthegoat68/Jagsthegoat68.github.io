Computers in 10's

By Jeevan and Jaideep

First IPad is released to the public

In 2010, The iPad combines many of the popular capabilities of the iPhone, such as built-in high-definition camera, access to the iTunes Store, and audio-video capabilities, but with a nine-inch screen and without the phone.

Facebook Purchases Instagram

Instagram, an image-sharing and social networking application, is purchased by Facebook for nearly $1 billion.

apple pay is released

The Apple Pay mobile payment system is introduced into Apple’s product ecosystem. Initially only available for the iPhone 6 and 6 Plus, iWatch, iPad Air 2, and iPad Mini 3, many major banks and credit card companies participated in the Apple Pay system.

apple watch is released

Building a computer into the watch form factor has been attempted many times but the release of the Apple Watch leads to a new level of excitement.

siri is announced

Siri is introduced as a built-in feature with the Apple iPhone 4S smartphone in October. A voice-activated personal assistant, Siri could “understand” natural language requests and also adjust the information it retrieved from the web by learning user tendencies and preferences
